Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never miss its delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground nuts
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. In a bowl.mix the sugar and nuts
  3. Toss the applea in the sugar-nuts mixture
